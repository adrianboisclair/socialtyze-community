California Pizza Kitchen

Community Management

overview / introduction

California Pizza Kitchen, commonly known as CPK, is a casual dining restaurant chain with more than 200 locations, which is known for its California style, and non-traditional pizzas.

Socialtyze partnered with CPK to provide community management and overall social strategy, including photography shoots, monthly data analysis, targeted paid Facebook media, and social app development, to not only help grow the CPK community, but to assist in the launch of their system-wide rebrand of Next Chapter.

Our Strategy

community

Our content strategy for CPK’s social channels (Facebook, Instagram and Twitter) was centered on the types of content specific audiences respond well to on each platform. The food photography was upgraded, tone was identified, and we also introduced a ‘Lifestyle’ element.

Facebook Paid Media

Apps

National Pizza Month: Guess the Pizza App

During National Pizza Month in October, CPK was to be featured on TNT’s new show On The Menu, a show that featured amateur chefs competing to create dishes for U.S Eateries, and have the winning dish placed ‘On the Menu’.

CPK came to Socialtyze to create a fun and engaging app to raise awareness for the show and the winning Korean Barbecue Pizza. We created the Facebook App ‘Guess the Pizza’, which allowed users to choose from a list of ingredients to guess what the new pizza would be.

The campaign resulted in valuable data on the CPK Facebook demographic, new user emails, and an average play time of 2 minutes 32 seconds. The winner won free CPK for a year.

Valentine’s Day: What We Do for Love

To promote CPK’s Valentine’s Day prix-fix menu as well as gain Loyalty Member sign ups, we created a microsite for fans to tell us what THEY do for love! We featured favorites on CPK’s social channels and, as part of an influencer marketing push with our sister company StoryCafe, leveraged celebrities from ABC’s The Bachelor to help get the word out.

Mother’s Day: Dear Mom Sweeps

CPK’s Dear Mom sweepstakes allowed fans to post selfies celebrating their moms for a chance to win a trip to Las Vegas. The response was a heartwarming gallery of positive, inspirational stories about mom. CPK partnered with Coke and the MGM Grand Las Vegas on the grand prize package.
